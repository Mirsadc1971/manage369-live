Celebrating Glenview's Distinctive Village Character

Named for the scenic views of its natural glen, Glenview has grown from its 1899 founding into one of the Midwest's most distinguished residential communities. The village's thoughtful development preserves its natural beauty while embracing modern amenities, from the elegant shops and restaurants at The Glen to the historic charm of downtown Glenview Road. Our headquarters location on Patriot Boulevard places us at the heart of this dynamic community, where we witness daily the pride residents take in their neighborhoods and the care they invest in their properties.

Glenview's unique character shines through its diverse neighborhoods—from the stately homes near Glen Oak Country Club to the contemporary communities surrounding The Glen, each area reflects the village's commitment to quality and livability. The community's exceptional educational foundation, anchored by the renowned Glenbrook South High School, attracts families who value academic excellence, while convenient Metra access connects residents to Chicago's professional opportunities in just 45 minutes. This perfect balance of suburban tranquility and urban connectivity makes Glenview properties particularly valuable investments requiring specialized management expertise.
