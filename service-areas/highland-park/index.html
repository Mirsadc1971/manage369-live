Cultural Heritage & Community Character

Founded in 1869 and incorporated as a city in 1889, Highland Park has evolved into one of America's most culturally rich communities. The city's name reflects its elevated lakefront position and the Scottish heritage of many early settlers, creating a foundation of excellence and community pride that continues to influence development and property management approaches today.
