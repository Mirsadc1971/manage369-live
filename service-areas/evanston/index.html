Dynamic City Character & Diversity

Founded in 1863 and incorporated as a city in 1892, Evanston was named after John Evans, one of Northwestern University's founders. This academic heritage continues to influence the city's character today, creating a unique environment where intellectual curiosity, cultural diversity, and urban sophistication converge to create exceptional opportunities for property management and community development.
