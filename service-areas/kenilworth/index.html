Historic Heritage & Distinguished Character

Founded in 1889 by Joseph Sears as a planned community designed to exemplify the finest in residential development, Kenilworth was conceived as a model suburb that would showcase the highest standards of American community planning. This visionary approach established design principles and community standards that continue to influence every aspect of village life today.
