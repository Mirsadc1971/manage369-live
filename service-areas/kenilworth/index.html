Historic Heritage & America's Most Exclusive Community

Founded in 1889 by Joseph Sears as a planned community designed to exemplify the finest in residential development, Kenilworth was conceived as America's first planned suburban community that would showcase the highest standards of community planning, architectural excellence, and social refinement.
