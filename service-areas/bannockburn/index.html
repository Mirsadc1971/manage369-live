Rich History & Environmental Heritage

Incorporated in 1924, Bannockburn was established with a unique vision of creating a residential community that preserved the area's natural beauty while providing exclusive living opportunities. The village's founders, including the legendary landscape architect Jens Jensen, designed a community that would work with rather than against the natural landscape, a philosophy that continues to guide development decisions today.

Jens Jensen's Lasting Influence

The renowned landscape architect Jens Jensen played a pivotal role in Bannockburn's development, designing landscapes that celebrated native prairie and woodland ecosystems. His influence is evident throughout the community in the preservation of native plantings, natural drainage patterns, and integration of built environments with natural systems.

This design philosophy influences contemporary property management companies in Bannockburn, as all maintenance and improvement activities must respect Jensen's ecological principles while meeting modern functional requirements. Understanding these design principles ensures property management decisions support rather than conflict with the community's environmental heritage.
