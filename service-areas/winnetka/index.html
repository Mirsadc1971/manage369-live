Distinguished Heritage & Architectural Excellence

Incorporated in 1869, Winnetka derives its name from the Potawatomi phrase meaning "beautiful place," a designation that continues to define the community's character today. The village's founders established a planned community with wide boulevards, generous lot sizes, and architectural standards that continue to influence development and property management approaches throughout Winnetka.
